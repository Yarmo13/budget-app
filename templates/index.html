Tracking Start Date

Set when you started tracking expenses to automatically prorate your monthly budgets. For example, if you set your budget to $500/month and start tracking on the 15th, the app will adjust your budget to reflect only the remaining days in the month.

Current tracking start date: Not set

💡 Tip: Once set, budgets for the month you started tracking will be automatically prorated. Months before the tracking start date will show full budgets. Months after will show full budgets too.
